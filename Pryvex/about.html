About PRYVEX

Founded in 2024 by Humphery Kufakwejeri, we are a Zimbabwean technology startup on a mission to close the gap between real-world problems and working software.

Our Mission

"Transforming ideas into impact, with tech that's as bold as your vision."

We develop practical, cost-effective digital solutions built for the Zimbabwean context, ensuring every product solves everyday challenges.

Our Vision

To become the leading provider of affordable, high-impact digital solutions across Sub-Saharan Africa — empowering communities through technology that works.

From Harare to the continent — building digital futures.

Our Founder

Humphery Kufakwejeri

Founder & Lead Developer

Information Technology student with a sharp eye for practical problem-solving. He founded PRYVEX to address the shortage of affordable, locally-relevant digital tools in Zimbabwe. His philosophy: technology should work for people, not the other way around.
